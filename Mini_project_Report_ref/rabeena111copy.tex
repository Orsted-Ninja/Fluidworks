\section{Chapter Details}
The report is organised as follows: In order to fully understand the background of keyphrase extraction, chapter two presents the related works which includes a literature review of the extraction mechanisms so far developed. Chapter three gives an overview of neural network and backpropagation learning method. From chapter four onwards, different stages of the proposed keyphrase extraction method are described with suitable block diagrams and algorithm. Chapter four has the document preprocessing, chapter five, the candidate phrase identification and chapter six, the different features used for describing phrases. Chapter seven clearly describes how training and testing is being done in the neural network. Chapter eight gives the testing strategies and results obtained and chapter nine the summary of obtained results. The last chapter, ten, give the conclusion and futureworks.

\chapter{Conclusion \& Future Work}

The proposed method for keyphrase extraction was described and implemented. The method uses neural network for training and classification. Candidate phrases are giving weights depending on their features and these are given to the network for training and testing. An algorithm is designed for selecting the K top ranked phrases as keyphrases. Authors are giving the right to determine the number of keyphrases, say K. The work is extentable and modifiable. As I mentioned in the abstract, it is possible to include or exclude the features, which determine the results.
